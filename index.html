Bachelor of Science in Plant Protection

Our Bachelor's program provides a solid foundation in plant biology, entomology, pathology, and integrated pest management. Students learn to identify and manage plant diseases, pests, and environmental stressors. Practical fieldwork and internships provide hands-on experience in real-world settings.

Master of Science in Plant Health

Our Master's program delves deeper into specialized areas of plant protection. Students have the opportunity to engage in advanced research projects, focusing on topics such as disease resistance breeding, biocontrol strategies, and sustainable agriculture practices. Graduates are prepared for leadership roles in research, industry, and academia.

Ph.D. in Plant Protection and Pathology

Our Ph.D. program offers intensive research training for aspiring plant protection scholars. Doctoral candidates work closely with faculty mentors to contribute groundbreaking research to the field. Graduates emerge as experts who drive innovation, influence policy, and shape the future of plant protection.
