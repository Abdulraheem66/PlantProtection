Professor Dr. Muhammad Kamran

Position: Chairperson, Plant Protection Department

Email: chairperson@gudgk.edu.pk

Professor Dr. Muhammad Kamran is a dedicated scientist and educator with a passion for advancing the field of plant protection. With over two decades of experience, he has led numerous research projects, authored influential publications, and inspired countless students.

Dr. Kamran holds a Ph.D. in Plant Pathology from a prestigious institution and has since then made significant contributions to the understanding of plant diseases and their management. His work in developing disease-resistant crop varieties has garnered international recognition.

As the Chairperson of the Plant Protection Department, Dr. Kamran is committed to fostering an environment of innovation, collaboration, and excellence. He believes in the power of education and research to address critical challenges in agriculture and environmental sustainability.

Dr. Kamran's leadership has resulted in partnerships with industry leaders, government agencies, and research institutions, all of which contribute to the department's impact on global plant health management. Under his guidance, the department continues to thrive as a hub of cutting-edge research and education.

Outside of his professional pursuits, Dr. Kamran enjoys , gardening, and mentoring young scientists. His dedication to plant protection and his vision for a greener future make him an invaluable asset to our department and the wider community.
