Current Research Areas

  • Biological Pest Control: Investigating the use of natural predators and beneficial organisms to manage pest populations sustainably.
  • Disease Resistance Breeding: Developing plant varieties with enhanced resistance to common diseases through advanced genetic techniques.
  • Integrated Pest Management: Creating comprehensive strategies that combine various pest control methods to minimize environmental impact.
  • Climate Resilience: Studying how plants adapt to changing climate conditions and developing strategies for improving their resilience.
  • Precision Agriculture: Exploring the use of technology to monitor and manage plant health on a micro scale, optimizing resource use.

Research Impact

Our research findings contribute to the advancement of plant protection on a global scale. Through collaborations with agricultural industries, government agencies, and international research networks, our department plays a pivotal role in shaping sustainable agricultural practices and safeguarding food security.

Research Excellence

Our commitment to research excellence drives us to explore novel strategies for pest and disease control, develop disease-resistant plant varieties, and pioneer environmentally friendly solutions. Our research findings are regularly published in esteemed scientific journals and shared at international conferences.
